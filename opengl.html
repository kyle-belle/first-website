Reasons for choosing opengl.

I choose Opengl not only because it is cross platform but also because in almost no case have I seen Directx(D3d) 9-11 to be faster. From here on if I do learn another api it will most definitely be the Vulkan api, the successor to opengl, which, ALREADY, has proven itself to be faster in most cases than Directx 12 while still maintaining it's cross platform advantage and better yet it supports windows 7 through to windows 10 meanwhile Directx 12 only supports windows 10 (which some people avoid).
